Sobre o Filme

Jurassic World 3 might still be a year away from its June 2022 release, but we've already been treated to the first footage from the movie. If you've been to see Fast & Furious 9 at an IMAX screening, you might have been confused by the movie starting in the Cretaceous period. Don't worry though, the Fast series might have gone to space, but they haven't time-travelled... yet. It's actually the start to Jurassic World: Dominion that you'll have seen which features a T-Rex and a Giganotosaurus going head-to-head. The full footage hasn't made its way online just yet, but you can see a teaser below.
